My favorite movie is "The Shawshank Redemption". It is a story about a man named Andy Dufresne who is sentenced to life in prison for a crime he didn't commit. Over the years, he befriends a fellow inmate named Ellis Boyd "Red" Redding and together they plan a daring escape.

Actors

  • Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne
  • Morgan Freeman as Ellis Boyd "Red" Redding
  • Bob Gunton as Warden Samuel Norton
  • William Sadler as Heywood
  • Clancy Brown as Captain Hadley

Quotes

"Get busy living, or get busy dying." - Andy Dufresne

"Hope is a good thing, maybe the best of things, and no good thing ever dies." - Ellis Boyd "Red" Redding

"I guess it comes down to a simple choice, really. Get busy living or get busy dying." - Andy Dufresne
